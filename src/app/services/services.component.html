Live Event Painting
 
Perfect for weddings! I will show up to your event and paint a portrait of the requested person or persons in acrylic paint on a canvas size of your choosing. I will also include the venue background. I will need a small space at the venue to set my easel. I will arrive early to block in the venue space and background and begin paint the subject/subjects when the even starts. If I do not finish by the end of the event or if the client would like revisions I will take the painting back to my studio and deliver the painting to the client at a later date. Clients can request up to three revisions if needed. I do not offer framing services.
Calculate the Painted Portrait price at the size you would like and add $150.
